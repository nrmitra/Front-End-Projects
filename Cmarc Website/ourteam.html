About Chinmoy

Chinmoy has been pivotal in C MARC’s journey, contributing significantly from its early days. He played a key role in establishing a comprehensive and diverse panel across regions, ensuring that C MARC’s syndicated CPR service attained the statistical rigor needed for actionable insights. With over 45 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, Chinmoy has seen pharmaceutical brands navigate various business cycles, directly contributing to the success of numerous major brands. This extensive experience makes him a valuable source of insights into effective strategies and product life cycles.

He holds a bachelor's degree in science and an MBA from Calcutta University.

About Neel

Before joining C MARC, Neel was part of the Emerging Markets division at Dr. Reddy's, where he managed market research initiatives across Russia, the CIS countries, South Africa, and other developing regions. Prior to his role at Dr. Reddy's, Neel worked as a technology analyst at BlackRock in Singapore.

Neel holds a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, and an MBA from the Indian Institute of Management, Bangalore (IIM B).

About Dr. Haresh

Dr Haresh has over 30 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry. With stints across major pharma companies such as Novartis, Lupin, Unichem and Torrent. He held various managerial roles in these firms - mainly overlooking commercial excellence and marketing strategies.

He holds a bachelors degree in sciences from the Mumbai University and a Masters degree in sciences from the University off Baroda.

About Jaideep

Jaideep brings over 20 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry. Before joining C MARC, he held managerial roles at Alkem Laboratories and Alembic Pharmaceuticals, where he oversaw business development and marketing strategies

He holds a bachelors degree in pharmacy from Jadavpur University and an MBA from Calcutta University.

About Amiya

With over 35 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, Amiya has been an integral part of C MARC throughout his career.
