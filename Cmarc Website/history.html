Our history

Professor Chitta Mitra of Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta, with the support of Dr. Tarun Gupta, the then Managing Director of GSK Family Care, established the organisation in 1977 ‘to study the medical practitioners’ inclination in India’. Prior to his tenure at IIM C, Prof. Mitra .....

Until 1983, GSK was the exclusive subscriber of C MARC’s flagship service - Continuous Prescription Research (CPR). Subsequently, other pharmaceuticals began engaging with C MARC’s services, leading to an expansion of its panel and regional reach, thereby enhancing its capacity for in-depth prescription insights.

As of 2024, C MARC continues to serve major pharmaceuticals operating in India. With ~9,000 prescribers part of its panel

Institute of Pharmaceutical Healthcare Management & Research (IPHMR)

IPHMR was established in the mid-2000s by C MARC to serve as one of the best sources for managerial talent in pharmaceutical and healthcare management.

IPHMR was established as an extended expression of C MARC as a committed institution for providing specialized human resources as future leaders of the industry. Till date, the initiative has produced over 100 professionals who continue to be active professionals serving various managerial roles across the healthcare domain.
